On July 7, 2003, The Canadian RioTinto Alcan announced its intention to acquire French aluminum company Pechiney Through a public takeover bid. This led to new practices within the company’s culture, including a Bravo Award ceremony for top-performing employees.

notes: >- alt: >- credit: >- - kind: block template: chapter title: >- slug: >- ranks: self: 2 data: layout: no_background text: >-

Following an analysis of the criteria involved in this trophy awarding process, «Trophy Generator» is a software that generates trophies reflecting the employee’s individual performance and status. This approach considers labour through scientific and mathematical modeling and raises concerns about the impact of such quantification on workers individuality and autonomy.
