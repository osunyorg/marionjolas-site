On July 7, 2003, The Canadian RioTinto
Alcan announced its intention to acquire
French aluminum company Pechiney
Through a public takeover bid. This led
to new practices within the company’s
culture, including a Bravo Award
ceremony for top-performing employees.


Following an analysis of the criteria
involved in this trophy awarding process,
«Trophy Generator» is a software
that generates trophies reflecting the
employee’s individual performance and
status. This approach considers labour
through scientific and mathematical
modeling and raises concerns about the
impact of such quantification on workers
individuality and autonomy.

Generated and cast trophies

This project had the opportunity to be
exhibited in Z33 Museumin Hasselt, as part of
«Technogeographies, fields of industry»exhibition.
